Dawn

Sunlight spills across the sill
and wakes the dreaming room.

Nightfall

Crickets sing to the moon
while shadows braid the sky.

Memory

I found your note between the pages
a pressed leaf, still green.

Autumn

Leaves whisper in the gallery
framed in antique gold.

Poem of the Month

In quiet stacks the secrets keep,
Ivy dreams and parchment sleep.
A golden rhyme for hearts to steep.
